Hi! We're ACCESS5.

We are a company dedicated to improving the experience of using public transportation for people with disabilities.
One of our solutions is TRANSPORT5, a smart bus stop for blind people.

Our values

Honesty

We want to be honest with our consumers and earn their trust, since trust is the basis of any relationship.

Clarity

One of our priorities is to be clear, since our work philosophy is simplicity. All communications from our side will be clear, with the truth upfront and without wanting to entangle the client.
